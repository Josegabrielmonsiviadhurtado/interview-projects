# Retail Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to identify trends, top-performing products, and business opportunities.

## Business Questions
- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- What are the monthly sales trends?
- Which categories perform best?
- What recommendations can increase sales?

## Tools Used
- Python
- SQL
- Power BI
- Tableau

## Project Status
✅ Completed

## Key Findings

### 1. Top Revenue Category
Electronics generated the highest revenue with total sales of $156,905, followed closely by Clothing and Beauty.

### 2. Monthly Sales Trends
Sales peaked in May 2023 with total revenue of $53,150. Strong performance was also observed in October and December.

### 3. Product Performance
Clothing recorded the highest number of units sold, while Electronics generated the highest revenue.

### 4. Customer Insights
Female customers generated slightly higher total sales than male customers.

## Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory and marketing efforts for Electronics products.
- Analyze the factors behind the strong sales performance in May and replicate successful strategies.
- Promote cross-selling opportunities between Clothing and Electronics categories.
- Develop targeted campaigns for female customers, who generated slightly higher revenue.
